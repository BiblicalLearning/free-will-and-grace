## ğŸ’¡ Key Concepts

### The Human Condition

- Free will is a giftâ€”essential for genuine relationship
- But free will naturally inclines toward sin
- We are free to choose, but not free from our nature

### What Grace Really Is

| Wrong Understanding | Right Understanding |
|---------------------|---------------------|
| Grace received after forgiveness | Grace is unmerited favor despite failure |
| Something we earn | Something freely given |
| A reward | A gift |

### Law vs. Grace

| The Law Could | The Law Could Not |
|---------------|-------------------|
| Show us what sin is | Remove the desire to sin |
| Reveal God's standard | Give us power to meet it |
| Condemn sin | Transform the sinner |
| Point to our need | Be the solution |

### Old Covenant vs. New Covenant

| Old Covenant | New Covenant |
|--------------|--------------|
| Repeated yearly | Once for all |
| Blood of animals | Blood of Christ |
| Covered sin temporarily | Removed sin eternally |
| Priests stood continually | Christ sat downâ€”finished |

### Under Law vs. Under Grace

| Under Law | Under Grace |
|-----------|-------------|
| Obey to be accepted | Accepted, therefore obey |
| Human willpower | Spirit's power |
| External commands | Internal transformation |
| Fear of punishment | Love for the Father |
